## Overview

OneTest's test management system helps you organize, track, and maintain your test cases efficiently.

  <Card

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Test Management">
    Access the Test Management section from the main navigation
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Your First Test">
    Click **"+ New Test"** to create your first test case
  </Step>

  <Step title="Organize with Folders">
    Create folders to organize your tests by feature or module
  </Step>

  <Step title="Run Tests">
    Select tests and click **"Run"** to execute them
  </Step>
</Steps>
